Wildlife Conservation

Wildlife conservation is the practice of protecting animal species and their habitats to ensure biodiversity and ecological balance.

Why It Matters

  • Preserves biodiversity and ecosystem health
  • Supports sustainable natural resources
  • Protects endangered and threatened species

Major Threats to Wildlife

  1. Habitat destruction and deforestation
  2. Climate change and pollution
  3. Illegal hunting and poaching

How You Can Help

You can contribute by:

  • Supporting conservation organizations
  • Reducing plastic and carbon footprint
  • Spreading awareness and education
